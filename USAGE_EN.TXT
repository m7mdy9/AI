## Description

This project is a website made for a national competition, it's purpose is to explain Artificial Intelligence in a new and creative way that allows users to experience it in 4 different languages.

## Features

This site features multilpe things that include:

- 4 Support languages (en, ar, es, fr)
- Full mobile compatability.
- Smooth scrolling with a scroll wheel, alongside GSAP based animations that occur on load and on scrolling.
- A stylish title icon.
- A small icon top right in the translated sites to go back to the language selector page.
- Slick animations and a modern design, in new and creative ways.
- A section where a model of an RTX disassembles as you scroll down and each part is explained, with the option to turn that 3D Animation off.
(Disabled on mobile devices for hardware reasons)
- Cards that include essential information regarding the Evolution of Artificial Intelligence.
- The site includes Pictures that fit the context.
- Font, font sizes and colors that are easily readable, and compatible based on aspect ratio.
- If you resize while viewing the site on Desktop the site changes it's layout in order to make it look the best and fit correctly.
